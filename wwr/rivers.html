The best rivers for Rafting

As we know that you are passionate about living adventures and enjoying nature, we show you 3 rivers that are located in the country of Colombia where you can live unforgettable experiences and enjoy a beautiful landscape while practicing rafting.

Fonce River

The Fonce River was the first to encourage Colombians to risk adventure. Thanks to its turbulent waters, in its category 3 and 4 rapids, Colombians discovered the passion for rafting, thus giving us that excitement to travel its rivers and be able to master it with the oars and the boat.

Chicamocha River

The Chicamocha river is for people who arrive determined to live an extreme and complete adventure that allows them to enjoy one of the most beautiful landscapes in Colombia. Black River

the Black river named for its dark waters that generate dozens of eddies and in winter times reach their highest level of growth, it is located near the municipalities of Nimaima and Tobia where an adventure industry developed that made the black river A must visit destination.
