About Cartagena page

There are 4 things that you must know if you want to delve into this magical world that Cartagena contains, which are its history, the Cartagena citizen, tradition and gastronomy.

In its history we will be able to know the origin of Cartagena together with its location, we will know a little about how from colonial times it could become one of the most important ports, we will also know a little about the origin of its name and an additional fact is that It was declared a Historical and Cultural Heritage of Humanity for being an iconic city during the Spanish colonial history.

The Cartagena citizen stands out for his joy and good spirits which he reflects at all times, music is an important part of the routine of a Cartagena citizen, so when you visit Cartagena you will know that he is happy because you see him from his house listening to music from the Caribbean region, to know about the Cartagena citizen is to know how they live in this city since their contribution is significant.

Knowing the traditions of Cartagena is understanding how customs are handled in this city and at the same time makes us feel more involved in their environment, within some traditions we can find the independence festivities which begin on November 11 and last a week which It is full of dance costumes and a lot of popular creativity.

Cartagena gastronomy has a lot of variety for being a coastal city, from its popular origins we can find typical foods such as the Fried Fish, which contains a crispy fried fish, coconut rice and patacones, accompanied by a lemonade will be a delight for your palate, the Cazuela de Mariscos which is a seafood soup that contains shrimp, squid and clams and finally the Ceviche which is one of the most desirable seafood dishes, especially as an appetizer or appetizer.

What to do in Cartagena page

Cartagena has many places to visit since it is a place full of diversity, here you can find from places that go back to history to modern places since it has these two facets, below, I show you 4 places that you must visit when you are in Cartagena.

Going to the beaches of Cartagena is something that you should do when you visit Cartagena since being a coastal city this is one of its most striking tourist attractions, some of the most touristic beaches are Playa Blanca, Laguito, Boca Grande, Castillo Grande Y La Boquilla, since the sun is going to be your best company, we recommend you bring your sunglasses, hat, sunscreen and an excellent attitude to enjoy the waves.

You can also visit the walled city that is located in the center of the city, this place is filled with colonial architecture that combined with the warmth of the city builds a magical atmosphere, to be able to take a tour of the walled city you can get on a carriage that will take you through all the streets and together with the tour guide you will be able to learn a little about the history while you do the tour.

Other of the most prominent tourist attractions are the islands that surround the city, which are located in a small archipiélago called the Rosario Islands which is made up of 28 islands, some of the ideal islands to visit is Pelican Island, a destination exclusive that can be reached by private yacht, also Isla Grande, Isla del Sol or El Encanto, which also offer the beauty and nature that characterizes the paradisiacal Colombian Caribbean.

Last but not least, for your visit to Cartagena to be complete, it is ideal to visit all those sites that fill us with its history. Among some of these places we can find Plaza de Las Bóvedas which is a military building and served as a military barracks or prison during independence, El Convento de la Popa is the highest point, from its chasm you can have a panoramic view of the entire city and the Museum of the Inquisition shows you its most realistic part that can impress you, but it is one of the essentials to know an essential part of the history of Cartagena de Indias.
